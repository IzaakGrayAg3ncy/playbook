Messaging

  1. We are Authoritative
    Knowledgeable, confident, actionable. We position ourselves as an authority and trusted source in web3 marketing. Our content demonstrates deep industry knowledge and provides clear, actionable advice.
  2. We are Innovative
    The ideas company, high-level, pioneering. Our messaging highlights our innovative approach to marketing. We use ambitious storytelling to position ourselves as an industry leader.
  3. We are Storytellers
    Attention-grabbing, connected, authentic. We focus on emotional storytelling to build connections. Our content features collaborations and encourages transparency.

Voice

  1. We are Conversational
    Our content uses clear, straightforward language that is easy to understand. We explain complex topics clearly.
  2. We are Confident
    Our tone is assuring and we position ourselves as an authority. Our advice and insights demonstrate expertise.
  3. We are Forward-thinking
    Our language reflects our pioneering approach. Our content focuses on future trends and developing technologies.
  4. We are Community-focused
    Our messaging highlights collaboration, partnerships, transparency. Our content aims to foster connections.

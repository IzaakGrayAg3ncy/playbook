Connor Centuria

ChainCentric Founder

Profile

ChainCentric CEO and founder. Makes long-term strategic decisions. Closely involved in approving top priorities, outlining core strategy, adapting business plans, and pitching to key stakeholders. Directs holistic business focus and expansion.

Goals

Empower team members to excel. Prioritize long-term growth strategies. Champion continuous improvement company-wide. Cultivate partnerships with key stakeholders. Boost revenue, profitability and market share through strategic leadership.

Challenges/Pain Points

Making difficult decisions affecting funding, investment, and brand reputation. Setting the right pace of work. Managing loss of resources and rising business costs. Securing financial support. Balancing day-to-day tasks with long-term strategy. Guiding the company through uncertainty and setbacks. Motivating teams through challenges and change.

Product Strategist

Profile

In charge of brand growth through relationship building and strategic expansion. Leads market research and ensures the brand communicates its core values accurately. Manages brand development initiatives focused on reaching new audiences. Oversees messaging that conveys the essence of the brand.

Goals

Develops compelling brand campaigns. Builds strong brands through strategy and execution. Collaborates with creatives. Ensures branding aligns with marketing goals and plans. Helps companies define and express their brand identity. Drives brand consistency across initiatives.

Challenges/Pain Points

Ensuring brand consistency in campaigns. Navigating budget constraints. Alignining branding with marketing plans. Faces competition from other strategists and rival brands. Working to differentiate brand amid crowded marketplace. Struggles breaking through noise.
